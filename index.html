The vision, briefly

A future needs a story as much as it needs infrastructure. The Codex is an attempt at that story for Australia's space-age moment — strange, sincere, sometimes absurd on purpose, and openly licensed. Around it sits a design vision for how the project could one day organise itself, held to a simple discipline: say plainly what exists, what is being built, and what is still imagined.
